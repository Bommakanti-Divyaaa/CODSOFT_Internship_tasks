About This Project

📊

The Dataset

This model was trained on the classic Advertising dataset from the ISLR textbook — 200 real-world records of advertising spend across three media channels and the resulting product sales.

🔢

Features (Inputs)

  • TV TV ad spend in $k
  • Radio Radio ad spend in $k
  • News Newspaper ad spend in $k
🎯

Target (Output)

Sales — product units sold (in thousands). The model learns how each advertising channel contributes to sales and uses those learned weights to predict outcomes for new budgets.

🤖

The Model

A Multiple Linear Regression model was trained using scikit-learn. It fits a straight-line relationship between each advertising channel and sales — interpretable, fast, and effective on this well-behaved dataset.
